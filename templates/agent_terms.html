Agent Access Terms

Draft operating terms for pilot and subscription users. Review with a solicitor before relying on these commercially.

Service

Equiome provides access to property-tool leads, referral intent data, CRM tools, reports, and follow-up workflows.

Subscription

Agents may be charged a monthly subscription based on plan, territory, lead allowance, or agreed commercial terms.

No Guarantee

A lead is an enquiry or expression of interest. Equiome does not guarantee appointment booking, instruction, completion, revenue, or conversion.

Agent Responsibilities

  • Use lead data only for legitimate property-related follow-up.
  • Keep login credentials secure.
  • Do not export, resell, or misuse personal data.
  • Respect marketing consent and customer communication preferences.
  • Handle referral fee disclosure obligations transparently where applicable.

Data Protection

Agents accessing leads are responsible for handling personal data lawfully, securely, and only for the purpose for which access was granted.

Referral Opportunities

The platform may record EPC, mortgage, conveyancing, solicitor, or valuation referral opportunities. Commercial arrangements for those referrals should be agreed separately and disclosed where required.

Cancellation

Subscription cancellation, notice periods, refunds, and territory rights should be set out in the signed commercial agreement or order form.
